Slip Cast Ceramic Vase Series

Design

These are a series of ceramic vases that I designed and fabricated. The original design pieces were carved from modeling foam. A multipart plaster mold was created to pour liquid ceramic slip into. Once the ceramic slip sets up the pieces are removed from the mold, modified and fired to harden the ceramic material. Once the inital firing process happens the pieces can be glazed to add color and go through a final firing process. I created 50-55 unique sets. These are two examples.
